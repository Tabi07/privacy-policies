# Privacy Policy for Wordnest

**Last updated:** July 20, 2026  
**Version:** 1.0

Wordnest ("we", "our", or "the app") is a utility application. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use it.

## No Account or Login Required

Wordnest does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal registration information to use the app.

## Data Collection & Storage

Wordnest does not collect, transmit, or store personal information such as your name, email address, contact information, or location. 

All core application functionality occurs locally on your device. Any files, text, or content processed within Wordnest are read from and saved directly to your device's local storage and are never uploaded or transmitted to any external servers operated by us.

## Advertising & Third-Party Services

Wordnest uses **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process certain technical data to serve ads, including:

* Pseudonymous device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
* IP addresses and general device performance data

For more details on how Google processes data, please refer to:
* [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
* [Google AdMob Data Policies](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543)

## Permissions

Wordnest may request standard system permissions (such as storage access) solely to allow core app functionality and save outputs locally on your device. These permissions are used exclusively for user-requested operations.

## Children's Privacy

Wordnest does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13.

## Data Security

Since core operations remain on your device and no personal data is stored on our servers, your information stays under your direct control.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in functionality. Any updates will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
