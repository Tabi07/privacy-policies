Privacy Policy for PixShrink

Last updated: July 5, 2026
Version: 1.0

PixShrink ("we", "our", or "the app") is an image resizing and compression tool. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use it.

1. No Account or Login Required

PixShrink does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal registration information to use the app.

2. No Data Collection

PixShrink does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information about you. Specifically, the app does not collect:


Names, email addresses, or contact information
Device identifiers or advertising IDs
Location data
Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data


3. Image Processing and Storage

PixShrink is used to resize and compress images, either individually or in bulk. All image processing happens locally within the app. Images you choose to resize or compress:


Are read from your device's own storage
Are processed entirely on your device
Are saved back to your device's own storage


At no point are your images uploaded to, transmitted to, or stored on any external server, cloud service, or third-party system operated by us. We do not have access to your photos or files.

4. Permissions

PixShrink may request storage/photo access permissions solely for the purpose of letting you select images to process and save the resulting output back to your device. These permissions are used only for that functionality and for no other purpose.

5. Third-Party Services

PixShrink does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services. No data is shared with or sold to any third party.

6. Children's Privacy

PixShrink does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13, as the app does not collect data from any user regardless of age.

7. Data Security

Since PixShrink does not collect or transmit any personal data or images off your device, there is no server-side storage of your information that could be exposed in a data breach. All data remains under your own control on your own device.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app's functionality. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: mehtabwazier@gmail.com


This privacy policy applies to the PixShrink application, version 1.0 and above, unless superseded by a newer version of this policy.
